## 🎮 GAME INTEGRATION MAP

### **Match Games:**

1. **Hiragana Match** (`/kids-mode/hiragana-match`)
   - Match hiragana characters to pictures
   - Example: あ (a) → 🐜 (Ant/あり)

2. **Katakana Match** (`/kids-mode/katakana-match`)
   - Match katakana characters to pictures  
   - Example: ア (a) → 🍦 (Ice Cream/アイスクリーム)

3. **Memory Match** (`/games/memory-match`)
   - Match Japanese words to emoji + English
   - Example: りんご → 🍎 apple

4. **Vocabulary Match** (`/match-game/:category`)
   - Category-specific matching
   - Example: Food category matches all food words

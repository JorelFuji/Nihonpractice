## 🎮 **HOW TO USE**

### **Method 1: Floating Button** ⭐ (Recommended)

1. **Visit any page** on the site
2. **Look for the blue floating button** in bottom-right corner
3. **Click the button** → Menu appears
4. **Select a chart:**
   - あ Hiragana (blue)
   - ア Katakana (purple)
   - 漢 Kanji (orange)
5. **Chart opens in draggable modal**
6. **Use the chart** while on any page
7. **Drag to reposition** as needed
8. **Minimize** when not in use
9. **Close** when done

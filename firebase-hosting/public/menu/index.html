About These Games

しりとり (Shiritori) is a classic Japanese word game perfect for learning vocabulary and having fun with friends! オセロ (Osero) is the Japanese name for Othello/Reversi. Also known as リバーシ (Ribāshi), it's a popular strategy game in Japan.

Example: オセロをしましょう。(Osero o shimashō) - "Let's play Othello."

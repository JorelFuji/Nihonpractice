   - Used for Japanese words
   - Rounded, flowing style

2. **カタカナ (Katakana)**
   - 46 basic characters
   - Used for foreign words
   - Angular, straight style

3. **かんじ (Kanji)**
   - Chinese characters
   - Thousands of characters
   - Used for meaning

### **Numbers (すうじ):**
- **いち** (ichi) = 1
- **に** (ni) = 2
- **さん** (san) = 3
- **し / よん** (shi/yon) = 4
- **ご** (go) = 5
- **ろく** (roku) = 6
- **なな / しち** (nana/shichi) = 7
- **はち** (hachi) = 8
- **きゅう / く** (kyuu/ku) = 9

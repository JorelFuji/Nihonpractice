# 🎮 Color Quest Champions - Pokemon × Soccer × Godzilla Learning Game

## 🎯 **Game Concept**

**"Color Quest Champions" (カラークエストチャンピオンズ)** - A Pokemon-style adventure game where kids collect monster characters (like Pokemon and Godzilla), play soccer with sports legends (Ronaldo, Messi), and learn Japanese through colors, geography, and conversation!

---

## 🌟 **Main Features**

### **1. Pokemon-Style Monster Collection**
- Collect **Color Monsters** (each represents a color)
- Evolve monsters by learning Japanese
- Battle with color mixing
- Trade with friends

### **2. Soccer Mini-Games**
- Play with Ronaldo & Messi characters
- Score goals by answering questions
- Team up with color monsters
- World Cup tournaments

### **3. Godzilla Boss Battles**
- Giant monster battles
- Answer questions to attack
- Color mixing strategies
- Epic rewards

### **4. Comprehensive Learning**
- **Colors** in 4 formats (Kanji/Hiragana/Katakana/Romaji/English)
- **Color mixing** (Red + Blue = Purple)
- **Months** in Japanese
- **Geography** (Japan, North America, Texas)
- **SOV sentences** building
- **Conversations** in Japanese

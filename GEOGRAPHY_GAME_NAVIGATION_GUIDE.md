- The tile is in the **third row** of the grid
- On mobile, it may be further down
- Keep scrolling until you see the cyan/blue tile with "NEW" badge

#### **Check 2: Look for "NEW" Badge**
- Geography Game has a bright **yellow "NEW" badge**
- It's one of only 3 tiles with this badge (Adult Learning, Study Journal, Geography Game)

#### **Check 3: Search by Color**
- **Cyan/Blue gradient** is unique to Geography Game
- It's the only tile with this specific color combination

#### **Check 4: Stats Verification**
- At top of menu, check "Games: 6"
- If it says "5", the page may not have updated
- Try **refreshing** (F5 or pull down on mobile)

### **Tile Not Clickable?**
- Make sure you're clicking the **Geography Game** tile (cyan/blue)
- Not the **Grammar Game** (green) or **SOV Game** (teal)
- Check for рҹҺҜ Target icon

### **Page Not Loading?**
- Check internet connection
- Try: https://nihonselfpacepractic.web.app/geography-game
- Clear browser cache if needed
- Try different browser
